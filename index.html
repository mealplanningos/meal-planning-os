Plan Your Week
Know your week before you plan your food. A hectic Tuesday means quick dinner or leftovers — not a new recipe. Fill this in once and stop making last-minute decisions.
⚡ Anything you add here shows up in your meal plan as a reminder — so your schedule is always visible when you're deciding what to eat.
No-Decision Menu
Pick up to 3 Go-To and 3 Experimental recipes — that's it. The limit is intentional: fewer options means fewer decisions every week. Build it once, run on autopilot.
💾 Everything automatically saves. Your recipes will be here every time you come back. Add your own or browse the Recipe Library to get started.
Recipe Library
Stuck staring at a blank menu? Start here. Tested, realistic recipes — not fancy, just reliable. Find what fits your life and hit Add.
How this works: Find a recipe you like, hit "Add to My Menu," and it'll appear in your No-Decision Menu with all the ingredients already loaded — ready to use in your meal plan and grocery list. You can edit any recipe after adding it.
Your Meal Plan
Tap any slot to assign a meal. Your week's commitments show above each day so you can plan around them. Pick from your menu, name it yourself, or choose a category — whatever's fastest.
Grocery List
Your list builds itself. Plan your meals, grab your list, get out of the store faster. Staples and backup items are always at the bottom so you never forget the basics.
❄️ Freezer Inventory
Your freezer is your secret weapon. Log extra portions when you batch cook, pull them out on your worst nights. We surface the oldest stuff first so nothing gets buried and forgotten.
Frozen Meals
FIFO Method
