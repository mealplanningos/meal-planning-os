Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026
The short version: We collect your email to create your account. Your meal planning data is yours. Stripe handles all payments — we never see your card details. We don't sell your data, ever.

1. What we collect

Email address — to create and manage your account.

Meal planning data — recipes, meal plans, grocery lists, freezer inventory, and weekly notes you create in the app.

Payment confirmation — Stripe notifies us when payment is successful. We never receive or store your card number, expiry, or CVV.

2. How we use it

To run the app and sync your data across devices. To send transactional emails such as account creation and password resets.

3. How it's stored

Your data lives in Supabase, a secure cloud database, protected so only your account can access it. Payments are processed by Stripe, which is PCI-compliant.

4. Sharing

We don't sell, rent, or share your data with anyone. The only exceptions are the infrastructure providers that run the service: Supabase for data storage and Stripe for payments.

If Meal Planning OS is acquired by or merges with another company, your data may transfer to the new owner as part of that transaction. If that happens, we'll post a notice here and you'll have the option to delete your account.

5. Data deletion

Email steven@mealplanningos.com to request deletion of your account and data.

6. Cookies

We use a single session cookie to keep you logged in. No advertising or tracking cookies.

7. Changes

If we make material changes, we'll update the date at the top. Continued use of the app means you accept the update.
